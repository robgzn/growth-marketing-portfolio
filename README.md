# Growth Marketing Research Portfolio

## Overview

This repository contains my research project for the 100Hires hiring process.

For this assignment, I selected the topic:

**AI-powered SEO content production**

The goal of this repository is to collect high-signal sources from real practitioners, organize their content, and build a structured research base that could support a future playbook.

## Research Goal

This project focuses on understanding how experienced marketers and SEO professionals are using AI in real content workflows.

I prioritized sources that show:

- Real-world SEO experience
- Actionable content frameworks
- Clear opinions about AI-assisted content production
- Active publishing on LinkedIn, YouTube, podcasts, or industry websites
- Strong potential to support a practical playbook later

## Selected Topic: AI-Powered SEO Content Production

I chose this topic because it sits at the intersection of SEO, content strategy, and AI workflows.

It is highly relevant for modern growth teams because the challenge is not only publishing faster, but producing useful, credible, search-aligned content while maintaining quality and human judgment.

As AI Overviews increasingly replace traditional SERP clicks, content creators need frameworks that work across both traditional search and AI-powered discovery.

## Expert Selection Criteria

The experts in this repository were selected based on the following criteria:

1. Active practitioners running SEO/content projects for themselves or clients
2. Recent content from 2025-2026 on LinkedIn, YouTube, or podcasts
3. Specific focus on AI + SEO rather than general marketing
4. Measurable results documented through case studies or research
5. Multi-platform presence with at least 2 active channels
