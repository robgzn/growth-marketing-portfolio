### Post 3: Token Count and AI Context Windows

Date: 1 day ago

Tory discusses the concept of "token count" and AI context windows in content strategy. She advises:

*   Brands should not over-correct for AI bots by "chopping and chunking" content
*   The priority should remain on user experience, clear structure, and providing succinct, relevant answers
*   Writing for bots at the expense of humans is a mistake

Key insight: Optimizing for AI context windows should not come at the expense of human readability and UX.

### Post 5: Agentic Readiness Audits Using Agentic Browsers

Date: Approx. 5 days ago

Tory suggests a practical method for brainstorming and conducting "Agentic Readiness" audits using an agentic browser like ChatGPT Atlas. The approach:
