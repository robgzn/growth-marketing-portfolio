## Recent Posts

### Post 1: 5 Reflections on SEO in 2025 and 2026
Date: December 2025

Kevin reflects on the state of the SEO industry with 5 key observations:

1. Metrics are broken. The link between citations and revenue is unclear. With clicks dying, rank checking is coming back as a way to gauge if Google likes a site.

2. Complexity is high. LLM answers are probabilistic. Running the same prompt 5x produces different brand mentions.

3. The Gold Rush is FOMO-driven. Boards pressure C-suite for AI strategy. Everyone wants to be first-mover even without clear ROI.

4. SEOs are becoming Builders. For the first time, AI can create the product (content pipelines), not just recommend changes.

5. Growth > Channels. Winning teams in 2026 will mix Brand, PR, and Technical SEO into unified squads focused on outcomes.

### Post 2: Trust First Wins in AI Search
Date: February 2026

Kevin discusses the importance of trust as a core SEO strategy in the age of AI search. Key points:

- Position your brand in a way that fosters trust with users
- Small business status, expert involvement, or high customer satisfaction can all be trust signals
- Trust matters because users may encounter your brand before they even search
- When users recognize your brand as trustworthy, they select it as a credible source in AI answers

### Post 3: Brand Visibility is the Biggest Predictor of AI Chatbot Mentions
Date: March 2025

Kevin found that brand search volume is the single biggest predictor of how often AI mentions a brand in answers. Key findings:

- Brand building creates AI memory availability similar to mental availability for humans
- More media coverage and digital mentions give more prevalence in AI training data
- New content on rising topics tends to get LLM traffic, especially from Perplexity
- ChatGPT performs better for established topics
- Headings structured as questions work well for LLM visibility

Link: https://www.linkedin.com/posts/kevinindig_the-biggest-predictor-or-ai-chatbot-visibility-activity-7306408942442598402-WOrp
