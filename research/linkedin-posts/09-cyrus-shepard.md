### Post: Cyrus Shepards AI Optimization Playbook (Shared by Matt Diggity)
Date: November 2025

Cyrus Shepard revealed that AI optimization now makes up 30 percent of his consulting work, up from 0 percent in January, and he expects 70 to 80 percent by December. His playbook includes:

1. Audit content by type: Transactional pages are safe, informational with unique data can survive, generic informational blogs are dying
2. Add proprietary elements AI cannot replicate: Webinar libraries, interviews, custom data visuals, firsthand experience photos
3. Optimize homepage for AI parsing: Clear expertise signals, credentials, services directly on homepage
4. Track AI visibility, not just rankings: Weekly AI visibility reports using tools like Gumshoe AI
5. Accept the automation paradox: Use AI to assist, not replace. Curation matters more than production
6. Prepare for Google quality rater changes: AI-generated content with no human touch is at risk
7. Focus on images and detailed how-tos: Visual explanation survives AI summarization better
8. Do not chase AI Overview appearances blindly: Appearances have almost no click-through rate
