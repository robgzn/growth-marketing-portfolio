## Post 3: AI Overview Links in Search Console

**Date:** Approx. May 15, 2025

Glenn explains Google's AI Overview link tracking behavior in GSC:

- ALL links in an AI Overview are tracked as position #1 in GSC if the AIO ranks at the top of the SERP
- If the AIO appears elsewhere, ALL links in that AIO take on the position of the block
- This can cause confusion for site owners analyzing their GSC data
- He notes that Google's AI Overviews overlap with 54% of organic search results, and adaptation strategy varies by industry

Link: https://www.linkedin.com/posts/glenngabe_google-seo-ai-activity-7328829035591929856-z_di

## Post 4: AI Search Platforms and International SEO

**Date:** Approx. December 4, 2025

Glenn published testing results on how AI Search platforms handle translated content and multilingual prompts:

- Tested ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, Claude, and Copilot for international SEO handling

## Post 5: Continuous Site Auditing for Quality

**Date:** Various 2025 posts

Glenn advocates for ongoing site audits through the lens of broad core updates rather than waiting for an update to impact a site:

- Site owners should proactively audit content quality before an update hits
- Google's systems evaluate sites over time, so quality issues can be caught early
- Start with highly visible and low-quality content, as that's where Google gains most Navboost data
- Use a "kitchen sink" approach - identify and fix all potential quality problems
- Negative user interaction signals on high-traffic pages should be the top priority

Link: https://www.linkedin.com/posts/glenngabe_google-seo-reddit-activity-7329502735706304512-lLEm
