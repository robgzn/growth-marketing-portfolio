### Post 4: Long-Tail Keywords as LLM Interaction Proxies

Date: Approx. 1 month ago

Ben outlines a strategy for using long-tail keywords as a proxy for understanding how people interact with LLMs. His 7-step process using Semrush filters:

1.  Find long-tail keywords with specific personas in mind
2.  Filter by search intent (informational, commercial, transactional)
3.  Identify keywords with "how to" and "what is" patterns
4.  Map these to specific customer personas
5.  Create landing pages optimized for each persona
6.  Structure content to match LLM-friendly formats (questions, structured data)
7.  Monitor visibility in AI search results

Key insight: Long-tail keywords reveal how real people phrase questions, which is exactly how they will prompt AI assistants.

Link: https://www.linkedin.com/posts/benjamingoodey_using-long-tail-keywords-as-a-proxy-activity-7455...
