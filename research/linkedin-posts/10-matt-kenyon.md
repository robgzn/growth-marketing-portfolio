### Post 6: How I Use AI Tools for SEO Content

Date: March 2025

Matt shares his AI-powered stack for researching and writing top-of-funnel SEO content, claiming it can cut content time in half:

1.  Use Perplexity for search intent analysis based on Google SERPs
2.  Run a Perplexity deep research report on your topic
3.  Run another deep research targeting YouTube videos for authority content
4.  Run xAI Grok + Google Gemini deep research to fill gaps
5.  Feed all documents into Claude to synthesize into a comprehensive outline
6.  Write, edit, polish with your own voice and expertise
7.  Ask Claude for contextual internal linking recommendations
8.  Enhance the post with rich elements (quotes, tables, graphics)
9.  Create a content brief in Surfer and run it through
10. Copy missing keywords from Surfer and paste into Claude for insertion recommendations
11. Publish, rinse, repeat
