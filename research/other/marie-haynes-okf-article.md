# Marie Haynes - Open Knowledge Format (OKF) Article Analysis

**Article Title:** How Google's Open Knowledge Format affects AI search visibility
**Source:** Marie Haynes Consulting Blog
**URL:** https://www.mariehaynes.com

## Summary

Marie Haynes has been publishing extensively about Google's Open Knowledge Format (OKF) and how it impacts AI search visibility for websites. Her analysis combines years of algorithm research with practical optimization frameworks.

## Key Concepts Covered

### What is OKF?
Google's Open Knowledge Format is a structured data approach that helps AI search systems understand and surface website content in AI Overviews and SGE results.

### Why OKF Matters for SEO
- Traditional ranking signals are less relevant in AI-powered search results
- OKF-structured content is more likely to be cited by AI systems
- Brands that don't adapt risk losing visibility to competitors with better-structured data

### Marie's Optimization Framework
1. Audit existing content for structured data completeness
2. Implement organization and product schemas
3. Focus on E-E-A-T signals (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness)
4. Monitor AI Overview appearances using tools like Gumshoe AI

## Practical Applications
- Use Marie's OKF checklist for content audits
- Apply E-E-A-T optimization across all brand content
- Test AI visibility before and after schema implementation
