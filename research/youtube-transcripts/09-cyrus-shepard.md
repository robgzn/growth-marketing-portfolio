**Key Insights from YouTube/Podcast Content**

*   AI search has fundamentally changed the content landscape - generic content is over
*   Proprietary expertise, custom data visuals, and firsthand experience are the new ranking factors
*   Tracking AI visibility (citations, mentions, sentiment) is as important as traditional rankings
*   Homepage optimization for AI parsing is often overlooked by most sites
*   Content creation must shift from "helpful" to "uniquely valuable with proprietary signals"
*   Social and community presence builds the trust signals that AI search engines prioritize
