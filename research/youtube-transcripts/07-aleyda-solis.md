**Key Insights from YouTube/Podcast Content**

*   AI search has transformed SERPs into marketplace-like interfaces with Google Overviews, product packs, and UGC content
*   Traditional ranking obsession must shift to pixel visibility, feature inclusion, and click behavior tracking
*   Social platforms (Reddit, Instagram, TikTok) now drive significant organic traffic away from traditional websites
*   Aleyda created LearningAIsearch.com (launched July 2025) as a free roadmap for AI Search Optimization learning
*   Brand presence in SEO is no longer optional—it must be integrated into strategy from the ground up
*   The SEOFOMO newsletter and LearningSEO.io roadmap are essential community resources for staying current
