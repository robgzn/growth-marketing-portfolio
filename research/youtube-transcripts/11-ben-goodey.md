**Key Insights from YouTube/Podcast Content**
* Revenue-driven SEO strategy must start with understanding audience intent and business pain points
* Spicy Margarita focuses on B2B/SaaS clients using a methodology that prioritizes pathways to revenue over vanity metrics
* Ben's "How the F*ck" newsletter/podcast has built a community around practical, no-fluff SEO case studies
* Content optimization tools (Surfer SEO, AI-powered tools) should serve strategy, not drive it
* Top-of-funnel content should only be created when necessary for topical authority building
* Ben advocates for team cultures that prioritize mental health and sustainable work practices (4-day work week model)
* GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) is an emerging area where SEO professionals can expand their skill set
