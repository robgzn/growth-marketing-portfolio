## Key Insights from YouTube/Podcast Content
- Clicks are "empty calories"; measure impressions and visibility instead
- Brand presence on Reddit, YouTube, and forums shapes user trust
- AI should be used for drafting at scale (30 drafts instead of 30 articles)
- Offsite signals (PR, community engagement, citations) now drive AI search visibility
