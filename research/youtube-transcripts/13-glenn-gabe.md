**Video References**
Glenn Gabe is the President of G-Squared Interactive LLC (GSQi) and a long-time SEO consultant specializing in Google algorithm update recovery and AI Search visibility. He hosts the video podcast "SEO From The Front Lines" on YouTube, covering algorithm updates, AI Search trends, and important SEO news. Episodes are typically under 15 minutes and published multiple times per week.
